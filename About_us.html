Brazilian Flavour began in 2019 in Recife, Brazil. We wanted to make the freshest and best tasting versions of classic Brazilian dishes like Feijoada and Coxinhas. We have 2 locations, th e original Recife location and a second location in Toronto, Canada. We offer both in person pick up and online delivery. We always offer the best in terms of quality and freshness.
